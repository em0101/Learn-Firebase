Overview

The AH-64 Apache is a powerful and versatile attack helicopter known for its exceptional firepower and advanced technology. It plays a crucial role in various military operations, providing close air support, anti-tank capabilities, and reconnaissance.

Specifications

  • Primary Role: Attack helicopter
  • Crew: 2 (pilot and co-pilot/gunner)
  • Maximum Speed: 182 mph
  • Armament: 30mm M230 Chain Gun, AGM-114 Hellfire missiles, Hydra 70 rockets
